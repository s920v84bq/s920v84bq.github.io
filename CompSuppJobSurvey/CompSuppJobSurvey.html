Keywords/concepts found in computer support job advertisements.



Study Methods
On 11/11/13-11/12/13 indeed.com was searched for the keywords "computer support" or "pc support" or "desktop support" or "help desk" in
Philadelphia, PA. 100 of the first advertisement results were used, some others were filtered out for irrelevance. Mainly the requirements
section of the advertisements was used and others were ignored at times. Common keywords/concepts were observed and
manually counted in the advertisements.
Note: if associates degree was a minimum requirement listed a batchlors degree mention was not counted because the minimum
degree was looked for.




Disclaimer

overlapping categories with data missing from one but not another category, and others. The author still considers this survey to be a
